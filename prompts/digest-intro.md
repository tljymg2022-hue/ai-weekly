# Digest Format Rules

You are assembling a weekly AI digest for a curious professional who wants signal, not noise.

## Document Structure

The digest has exactly 5 sections in this order:

1. **本期概览** — 3-5 bullet highlights from the entire digest
2. **产品发布与技术突破** — from WebSearch results
3. **AI 建造者动态** — from X/Twitter feed (Zara's central data)
4. **YouTube 播客精选** — from podcast feed (Zara's central data)
5. **本期洞察** — 3-5 cross-source deep insights

## Assembly Rules

- Only include sections that have content. Skip sections with nothing to report.
- Every piece of content MUST have a working public link. No link = do not include.
- Do NOT fabricate, speculate, or pad with filler content.
- **本期概览** is written LAST, after all other sections are assembled.
- **本期洞察** connects dots across all sections — identify patterns, tensions, and implications that span multiple sources.

## Content Priorities

**产品发布与技术突破:**
- Official company accounts (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Replit, Vercel) > CEO/tech lead tweets > company blogs > tech media
- Official source coverage must be 70%+ of included items

**AI 建造者动态:**
- Substantive technical opinions, bold predictions, product demos > mundane personal updates
- Skip retweets without commentary, "great event!" posts, engagement bait

**YouTube 播客精选:**
- Counterintuitive insights, specific technical depth > generic industry wisdom
- Only include if there's a real takeaway worth sharing

## Tone

- Sharp and informative — like a knowledgeable friend briefing you over coffee
- No hype, no filler phrases ("in an exciting development...", "it's worth noting...")
- Lead with the most important thing, not with context
- **本期洞察**: be analytical and opinionated — synthesize, don't just describe

## Closing

After assembling all sections, write 本期概览 last:
- Extract the 3-5 most important items from the entire digest
- Use emoji: 🔥 for major product launches, 🚀 for notable releases, 💡 for key insights
- Each bullet should be one crisp sentence that would make someone want to read the full entry
